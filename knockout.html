The Tournament Stages

Draws and Group Stage

The group stage draw is made up of 32 teams that are drawn into eight groups of four with a restriction to ensure teams from the same nation are not drawn against each other. The teams are seeded into four pots based on a couple principles. Pot 1 will contain the reigning champions and the top seven ranked teams based on the coefficient rankings. Pots 2,3 and 4 are all seeded based on the coefficient rankings. Once teams are drawn into their groups they must finish first or second in order to advance to the knockout stages of the Champions League. The third-place team will advance into the round of 32 in the Europa League and the fourth-place team is eliminated from European competitions completely.

Knockout Stages

The knockout stages are made up of four rounds: the round of 16, quarter finals, semi-finals and the finals. Each round is played with a home and away game and the team leading on the aggregate score line over both the games advances to the next round.
